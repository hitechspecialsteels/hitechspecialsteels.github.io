About Hi-Tech Special Steels

Hi-Tech are commited to producing the highest quality products and, in occordance with our company policy, firmly believe that it is the responsibility of all staff to achieve exellence in the provision of both product and service.

Our materials are aimed at most global industrial markets such as;

  • Petrochemical
  • Gas
  • Valves and Fittings
  • Fasteners
  • Food processing

Certification

Concern with quality starts with the initial casting of the material itself and ends with the acceptance of the goods by the customer. To this end we carefully select our raw material suppliers to ensure full compliance with our requirements and copies of mill certificates and any independent labrotory reports are supplied with delivery of the material.

Accreditation

Our quality system is audited by the NQA. We have been accredited since November 1989 and all of our materials are supplied in accordance with the requirements of BS EN ISO 9002.
