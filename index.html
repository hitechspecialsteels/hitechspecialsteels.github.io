Welcome to Hi-Tech Special Steels Ltd

Hi-Tech Special Steels is a privately owned company, originally established in 1985 to provide customers with special products not available in general supply.

The successful development of cold drawn square and rectangular sections for the manufacture of valve bodies soon led to various additional products being added to our now extensive range of available items.

Our success is largely down to our focus on manufacturing a high quality product supported by a superior service.
